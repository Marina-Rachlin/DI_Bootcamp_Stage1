Pets

At Torquay Boutique, we understand that your four-legged friend is an important part of your family. That's why we happily welcome pets at our hotel, ensuring that both you and your furry companion have a memorable stay.To cover the additional cleaning and maintenance associated with accommodating pets, a charge of CHF 30 per night (excluding food) will be applied. This fee ensures that we can maintain the utmost cleanliness and comfort for both you and your beloved pet. We look forward to hosting you and your furry friend at Torquay Boutique, where your pet is treated with the same care and attention as any other guest.
